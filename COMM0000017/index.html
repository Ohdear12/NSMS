BMR System Availability during Event

All BMR sites in the impacted area were 100% available throughout the entirety of the event.

All dispatch centers in Nova Scotia were 100% available throughout the entirety of the event.

  • • RCMP Main & Backup - 100% Available

  • • EHS Main & Backup - 100% Available

  • • NS TIR Main & Backup - 100% Available

  • • HRM Main & Backup - 100% Available

  • • CBRM Main & Backup - 100% Available

1 wireline fibre cut was being repaired during event due to a vehicle accident the evening of April 18th on the
south eastern shore area of the province; impacting an HDR1 link between Halifax & Sydney.

  • • BMR Site Ecum Secum had a 1 hour 50 minute site trunking outage on the morning of Sunday, April 19th during the fibre
    splicing (the site was not in the immediate area of the shooting event)

Approximately -70% of talk group usage on the Londonderry site was law enforcement traffic on April 19th.
The remaining 30% was mostly EHS (NS Ambulance services).

  • • Other sites showed similar talk group usage throughout the event on April 19th.
